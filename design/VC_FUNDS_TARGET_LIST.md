## PREPARATION CHECKLIST

Before sending any application:

- [ ] **Pitch deck** (12-14 slides): Problem, solution, architecture, market, traction, team, ask
- [ ] **Executive summary** (1 page): Concise overview for initial outreach
- [ ] **Technical whitepaper** (optional): For deep-tech funds (Khosla, Radical, AIX)
- [ ] **Financial model**: 3-year projections, unit economics, use of funds
- [ ] **Demo / prototype**: Working product or detailed mockup
- [ ] **Team bios**: Highlight AI/ML expertise, entrepreneurial experience
- [ ] **Market analysis**: TAM/SAM/SOM with credible sources
- [ ] **Competitive landscape**: Differentiation from LLM wrappers, foundation models

> **Note:** AI VCs in 2026 are pattern-matching against Cursor, Glean, and Hebbia — not undifferentiated GPT wrappers. Lead with proprietary-data moat or sharp vertical wedge.
